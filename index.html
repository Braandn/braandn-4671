Privacy Policy (Discord Bot)

We value your privacy and ensure that your data is protected.

  • Sent Messages: For the purpose of our leveling system, every message you send in this server is registered. This does not mean the bot is storing the content of your messages, but rather the act of sending a message is noted for points accumulation.
  • Time Spent in Voice Channels: If you spend time in any voice channel, it's counted towards our leveling system. If you're in the AFK channel, this time is excluded and not tracked.
  • Interactions on the Server: Every interaction you make on our server, including reactions to messages and clicks on buttons, may be tracked by the bot. In some cases, this can count towards the leveling system.
  • Commands: Each command you input into the server is noted to monitor and ensure the bot is functioning correctly and to analyze which commands are most popular or if any need adjustments.
  • We do not share or sell any data with third parties.

If you have any concerns about your privacy, please reach out to us.
